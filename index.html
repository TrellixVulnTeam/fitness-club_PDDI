About Us

In 2015, our founder Ethan Thomas was at his favorite gym, after another great day of workout he realizes that the fitness industry needed something other than what he was receiving. Ethan realizes that there was an accessibility problem, fitness doesn't help you with just your physical health but it helps you with your mental health also, and Ethan was searching for the perfect and effective websites but couldn't find one, and that's why FitnessClub was founded. Here at FitnessClub, we provide value for our customers, we have some of the best pro trainers in the country, with a lot of experience who are providing you with good and quality content.
